Our Story

At the Encinitas Cafe, we have been serving comfort food with first class service to our local community for over 30 years. We have always been a place of warmth to our regulars and visitors alike.

Preceding The Encinita Cafe, our establishment was originally Fred’s Cafe in the 30’s, Zim’s Cafe in the 40's, then The Coffee Mill in 1960, before becoming The Encinitas Cafe in 1989.

Homestyle cooking

Our goal is to deliver a dining experience as if you were home with close friends and family. Come see why so many have made The Encinitas Cafe their Home Away From Home.

Featuring local art

Feel welcomed by a mural and rotating art pieces from Encinitas's own Kevin Anderson.

Dog Friendly

Your furry friends are always welcome to join us on our outdoor patio.
